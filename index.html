Description

Research
Description

Before I begin coding the story, I research as much valuable information as I can about your industry and your interests so I have a foundation/starting point when I begin designg the website.

Additional Details
Design Phase
Description

This is where your story begins to form. I like to design my websites and projects before I craft the functions and user inputs, much like an author would write an outline for the plot of the story then make adjustments as needed as the story unfolds.

Additional Details
Website Comes to Life
Description

The the basic design is in place. The information, functionality, and mobile responsiveness gets plugged in and the website really begins to come to life.

Additional Details
Testing
Description

At this point the website is basically complete. This is the last and potentially the most important part of building your story. This is where I put the website through rigorous testing so that I can try to detect any bugs that might have slipped through the cracks during the earlier phases to ensure I deliver you the best possible product.
